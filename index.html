Welcome to Winx Ink Studio

Born from the creative vision of a passionate female entrepreneur, Winx Ink Studio is the evolution of Pretty Little Winx—a beloved sanctuary for beauty and self-expression. What began as a haven for lashes and brows has blossomed into a destination for fine line tattoos, where delicate artistry and individuality take center stage.

Here at Winx Ink Studio, we believe tattoos are more than just ink on skin—they’re a celebration of your unique story. Specializing in fine line tattoos, we craft minimalist, timeless designs that reflect your personality with precision and grace. Whether it’s a subtle symbol, an intricate floral piece, or a custom creation, our artists are dedicated to bringing your vision to life with meticulous attention to detail.

As a proud female-owned studio in the heart of Texas, we’re here to empower and inspire. Winx Ink Studio isn’t just a place to get tattooed—it’s a space where creativity thrives, confidence grows, and community flourishes. We’re passionate about supporting our local community and creating art that resonates with the bold, the dreamers, and the free spirits who walk through our doors.

Studio Policies & Tips

From consultation to aftercare, we ensure a seamless tattoo experience.

At Winx Ink Studio, we believe in a collaborative approach to tattooing. We work closely with clients to design meaningful, delicate pieces. Our process includes a personalized consultation, precise execution, and detailed aftercare guidance.

Studio Policies

  • Deposits: All deposits are non-refundable. They secure your appointment and are applied toward the final cost of your tattoo.
  • Quotes: The fastest way to get a quote is to text us. Before reaching out, review the Services section on our website to answer any preliminary questions.
  • Consultation Details: Include all inspiration pictures and specify the placement of your tattoo when contacting us for a consultation. Be as specific as possible. For example:
    • Instead of: “I want my children’s birth flowers on my forearm.”
    • Try: “I have three children, and I want their birth flowers (daisy, daisy, and rose) on my forearm. I’d like a few filler flowers and minimal shading.”
  • Tattoo Style: We specialize in fine line tattoos, typically 3 inches or smaller with minimal shading.
  • Age Requirement: By law, we cannot tattoo anyone under 18 years of age, even with parental consent.
  • Response Times: For quote requests, we are typically available during evenings and weekends. Clients with active appointments take priority, but we will respond as soon as possible to accommodate your needs.

Tips for a Comfortable Session

  • Prepare for Your Appointment: Eat a good meal before your session to maintain energy levels. Stay hydrated and avoid alcohol or caffeine beforehand.
  • Wear Comfortable Clothing: Choose loose-fitting clothing that allows easy access to the tattoo area.
  • Bring Entertainment: Feel free to bring headphones, a book, or anything else to help you relax during the session.
  • Follow Aftercare Instructions: Proper aftercare is essential for healing. We’ll provide detailed instructions to ensure your tattoo heals beautifully.
  • Communicate Openly: If you have any concerns or questions during the process, don’t hesitate to let us know. Your comfort and satisfaction are our top priorities.
